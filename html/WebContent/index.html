Most people who experience cardiac arrest at home, work or in a public location die because they don't receive immediate CPR from someone on the scene. This is mostly due to lack of training and confidence from surrounding bystanders. Our system uses wearable technology to effectively teach CPR and save lives.

TEAM diVRse

diVRse consist of 8 passionate team members wanting to reach out to the rest of the world all with the aim of providing a better future. Virtual Rescuscitation is an improved method in teaching CPR.

We are using the Oculus Rift to simulate a virtual, 3-dimensional CPR training environment and tracking responder's actions using motion control with Myo while providing visual feedback.
